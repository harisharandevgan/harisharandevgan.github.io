About Me

Harisharan Devgan, born on 25 August 1974, is an Indian innovative farmer, entrepreneur and emerging public leader recognized for his contributions to organic farming since 2001 in Andhra Pradesh. He created innovative methods for large-scale organic pomegranate cultivation, leading to honors such as "Organic Farmer of the Year" and "Entrepreneur of the Year" in 2017. As a committed social worker, he educates rural farmers about sustainable practices, financial management, and soil health.
Increasingly seen as a political leader (as Kisan Neta), Harisharan advocates for agrarian rights and policy reforms, working to connect farming communities with policymakers. His efforts demonstrate a strong dedication to sustainable development, rural empowerment, and national progress.
